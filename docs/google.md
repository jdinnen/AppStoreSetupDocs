# Google Play Console Organization Account Setup Guide

## Overview
This guide outlines the requirements and steps for creating a **Google Play Console Organization Developer Account** for publishing apps under your business or organization.

---

## 1. Account Type
- **Personal Account**: For individual developers or hobbyists.
- **Organization Account**: For businesses, nonprofits, or government entities.
  - Requires business verification.
  - Must provide legal entity information and supporting documents.

---

## 2. Required Information
Before you begin registration, prepare the following:

### Organization Details
- **Organization Name** – Legal name as registered with your government.
- **Organization Address** – Physical business address.
- **Organization Phone Number** – Used for verification.
- **Organization Website** – Verified via Google Search Console.
- **D-U-N-S Number** – Unique business identifier from Dun & Bradstreet.

### Developer Profile Information
- **Developer Name** – Public name shown on Google Play.
- **Developer Email Address** – Public contact for users.
- **Developer Phone Number** – Public support contact (required for organizations).

### Account Owner Information
- **Contact Name** – Individual managing the account.
- **Contact Email Address** – Used for account notifications.
- **Contact Phone Number** – Used for verification.

### Verification Documents
You will need to upload proof of the following:
- **Organization Verification** – Business registration, incorporation certificate, or tax document.
- **Identity Verification** – Government-issued ID of the account owner.
- **Website Ownership Verification** – Done through Google Search Console.
- **Phone & Email Verification** – OTP-based validation during signup.

---

## 3. Registration Steps
1. Sign in at [Google Play Console Signup](https://play.google.com/console/u/0/signup).
2. Choose **Organization** as the account type.
3. Pay the one-time registration fee (USD $25).
4. Create or link a **Google Payments Profile** for your organization.
5. Enter your **D-U-N-S Number** and ensure all legal details match your documents.
6. Provide developer profile details (developer name, website, contact info).
7. Accept the **Developer Distribution Agreement**.
8. Submit your application for verification.
9. Complete verification steps:
   - Email & phone verification
   - Upload identity and organization documents
   - Verify website ownership (optional but recommended)
10. Wait for approval (usually a few days).

---

## 4. Best Practices
- Use a Google account dedicated to your organization (not personal Gmail).
- Ensure your **Payments Profile**, **D-U-N-S**, and **legal documents** have consistent details.
- Keep all contact information valid and up to date.
- Use a recognizable **Developer Name** that matches your brand.
- If monetizing apps, set up tax and payout details in the Payments section.

---

| Step | Duration |
